Institution Overview

Institution Details

Established in 1966, Z.P. High School serves Jonnavalasa and surrounding rural communities in Vizianagaram district. Under PM SHRI, the school is advancing vocational learning, digital classrooms, science labs and sports.

Our Mission

To deliver technology-enabled co-educational learning that balances classroom teaching with practical science, vocational exposure, physical wellness and community responsibility.

Our Vision

To serve as a model secondary school of excellence, showing how green skills, agricultural awareness and computational systems can shape well-rounded responsible citizens.

Core Values

Fostering discipline, environmental awareness, recycling, organic farming, digital literacy, gender equality and community values through inclusive education.

Centrally Sponsored Scheme

PM SHRI Model School

Z.P. High School Jonnavalasa was selected in the 2023-24 PM SHRI cycle. The centrally sponsored program upgrades school assets into a 21st-century model learning hub.

PM SHRI Model School Selection

Selected in 2023-24

The Ministry of Education selected Z.P. High School Jonnavalasa under the PM SHRI model school program, enabling infrastructure funding for science, sports, green campus work and digital classroom resources.
